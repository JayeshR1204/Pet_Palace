Exotic Shorthair

View Gallary
  • Origin: United States
  • Height: 8 to 10 inches
  • Weight: 4-7 kgs
  • Color: white, blue, black, red, and lilac.
  • Life-Span: 8 to 11 years
  • Personality: calm and friendly.
  • Scientific Name: lazy man's Persian


About This Pet

Exotic Shorthairs have a round, compact body with a broad chest and short legs. They have a large, round head with prominent cheeks, a short snub nose, and big round eyes that can be various colors. Exotic Shorthairs are known for their calm and gentle nature. They are generally easygoing, affectionate, and enjoy being lap cats.Exotic Shorthairs are known for their calm and gentle nature. -:    Personality    :-

Exotic Shorthairs are known for their gentle and calm demeanor. They tend to have a laid-back attitude and are not as active or energetic as some other cat breeds. Exotic Shorthairs are generally very affectionate cats. They form strong bonds with their owners and enjoy being in their presence. These cats are usually quite social and enjoy the company of their family members. They are generally good with children and other pets, making them well-suited for households with multiple animals. While not as active as some other cat breeds, Exotic Shorthairs still have a playful side.
